About Campus Stride

Campus Stride is Kenya's premier destination for high-quality doll shoes and slides. Founded in 2023, we specialize in creating beautiful, durable footwear for dolls of all sizes and types.

Our passion for craftsmanship drives us to use only the finest materials and attention to detail in every piece we create. Each pair of shoes is handcrafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work.

Based in Nairobi, we ship nationwide and aim to bring joy to doll collectors and enthusiasts across Kenya with our unique designs and exceptional quality.
