About Campus Stride

Campus Stride is Kenya's premier destination for high-quality ladies' shoes and slides. Founded in 2023, we specialize in creating beautiful, durable footwear for ladies of all styles.

Our passion for craftsmanship drives us to use only the finest materials and attention to detail in every piece we create. Each pair of shoes is handcrafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their work.

Based in RVIST Kabasis Main Gate, we serve every section of the campus and aim to bring joy to ladies with our unique designs and exceptional quality.
